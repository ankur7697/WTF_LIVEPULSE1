# Product Overview

WTF LivePulse is an operations dashboard for a chain of gyms.

The product is designed to answer four questions in real time:

1. Which gyms are busy right now?
2. Which gyms are generating revenue today?
3. Which gyms need attention because of anomalies?
4. How are trends changing across the network?

## Core User Experience

- The dashboard view shows a selected gym, its live occupancy, today revenue, active anomalies, and a live activity feed.
- The analytics view shows the selected gym's heatmap, revenue by plan, new vs renewal ratio, churn risk rows, and cross-gym revenue ranking.
- The anomalies view lists unresolved anomalies and allows warning-level anomalies to be dismissed after confirmation.
- The simulator lets you start, pause, and reset synthetic activity so the live system can be observed without manual data entry.

## Who This Helps

- Gym managers who need a fast operational view of occupancy and revenue
- Regional operators who want to compare gyms across a network
- Support teams who need to react to anomalies and capacity breaches
- Product reviewers who need a complete real-time demo with live updates

## Main Product Signals

- Live occupancy per gym
- Today's revenue per gym
- Activity feed of check-in, checkout, and payment events
- Active anomaly detection and resolution
- Churn risk based on member inactivity
- Cross-gym revenue comparison
- Heatmap built from recent hourly check-ins
